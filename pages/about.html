Meet the founder

The person behind
Clarix Co.

I'm Karthick P M — a full-stack developer, data analyst and AI engineer based in the UK. I founded Clarix Co because I saw too many businesses paying agency prices for generic, templated work that didn't actually solve their problems.

Before starting Clarix Co, I worked as a freelancer — building websites, Power BI dashboards and AI agents for businesses across the UK, India and the United States. Every project taught me something new, and every client pushed me to deliver something better than what came before.

UK Master's Degree UK Qualified

Postgraduate qualification from a UK university · Real UK industry experience · Based in the UK

Having a UK Master's degree and real UK work experience means I understand how British businesses think, what they expect, and how to deliver to UK professional standards. Clients in the UK aren't getting an offshore service — they're getting someone who lives and works here, understands the market, and is accountable to UK law.

At the same time, my background gives me a genuine understanding of the Indian and US markets — the payment systems, the user expectations, the design sensibilities. That's why Clarix Co can build for Razorpay as comfortably as Stripe, and design for a UK audience as easily as a global one.

I build everything from scratch. No templates. No shortcuts. Every project I take on gets treated as if my own reputation depends on it — because it does.
